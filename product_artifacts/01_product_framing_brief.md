# StayReady AI Product Framing Brief

Status: Draft for review

## Product Name

StayReady AI

## Category

Villa Concierge Operations Overlay

## One-Sentence Positioning

StayReady AI is a white-labeled, WhatsApp-first AI concierge and lead-conversion layer for premium staffed villa operators, starting in Jamaica, that helps teams respond faster, organize guest readiness, route service requests, and preserve the human luxury feel.

## Product Summary

StayReady AI turns fragmented guest conversations from WhatsApp-style messaging, website inquiries, email, and other channels into structured, staff-ready workflows for premium villa operators.

Jamaica is the launch wedge and validation market, not the long-term boundary. The product should be designed as a globally reusable concierge operations system for premium villa and staffed-stay operators, with Jamaica-specific workflows used first to create depth, credibility, and a repeatable playbook.

It helps villa teams capture and qualify direct leads, answer common questions from approved villa information, collect pre-arrival details, coordinate common service requests, support in-stay questions, route incidents, and follow up after checkout.

The product should not feel like an AI chatbot replacing hospitality staff. It should feel like a branded operating layer that helps human teams deliver smoother, faster, more organized concierge service.

## Primary ICP

Launch ICP: premium, concierge-heavy villa operators in Jamaica managing roughly 5-50 high-service villas.

Long-term ICP: premium villa, staffed-stay, and concierge-heavy luxury rental operators in other villa markets where guests expect high-touch service, pre-arrival coordination, local recommendations, service request handling, and human-led hospitality.

Priority geographies:

- St. Ann / Ocho Rios
- Montego Bay
- Negril
- Port Antonio

Priority operator types:

- Multi-property villa managers
- Managed rental-pool operators
- Luxury villa agencies
- Owner-managed villa portfolios with several properties

## Buyer

The primary buyer is the owner/operator, villa portfolio manager, luxury villa agency leader, or managed rental-pool operator.

## Primary Users

- Reservations manager
- Guest relations manager
- Concierge manager
- Operations/admin staff
- Villa/property staff who receive handoffs

## Core Problem

Premium villa operators receive guest inquiries and guest requests across fragmented channels such as WhatsApp, websites, email, phone, and OTA messaging.

Much of this communication is handled manually, which creates slow responses, missed direct leads, repeated questions, messy WhatsApp threads, incomplete arrival details, chef/grocery/dietary back-and-forth, transfer coordination gaps, excursion request confusion, in-stay support delays, incident response delays, weak post-stay follow-up, and unclear staff handoffs.

## Product Promise

StayReady AI helps villa teams respond faster, capture more direct leads, collect the right guest information earlier, organize guest requests across the stay journey, and route complex guest needs cleanly without replacing staff or existing systems.

## Strategic Positioning

StayReady AI should be positioned as:

- Jamaica-first launch wedge
- Villa-native
- WhatsApp-first
- White-labeled
- Human-in-the-loop
- Concierge operations overlay
- Lead-conversion and guest-readiness layer
- Globally extensible operating model

Jamaica-first means the product starts with a focused geography, local service patterns, and a sharp first customer segment. It should not imply that the product is only for Jamaica. Over time, the system should adapt to other premium villa markets by swapping in market-specific knowledge bases, service categories, escalation rules, partner lists, and brand voice controls.

StayReady AI should not be positioned as:

- Generic AI chatbot
- Hotel guest app
- PMS
- Channel manager
- Booking engine
- Payment platform
- Dispatch platform
- Supplier marketplace
- Full tour marketplace
- Replacement for human concierge staff

## Prototype Direction

The first prototype should be something that can be shown to villa operators to validate whether the workflow reflects real pain, whether operators would pilot it, and whether they would eventually pay for it.

The prototype should show the full villa guest journey in lightweight form, not just inquiry and pre-arrival.

The prototype should include:

- Guest-facing WhatsApp-style messaging
- Guest-facing website inquiry
- Lightweight operator dashboard
- Lead capture and qualification
- Approved knowledge-base response behavior
- Pre-arrival intake
- Airport transfer request capture
- Chef/grocery/dietary request capture
- Check-in/orientation support
- In-stay support for common questions
- Excursion/activity recommendation and request capture
- Incident handling and recovery workflow
- Human handoff queue
- Internal notes and AI-generated summaries
- Post-stay review/NPS and repeat-offer workflow
- Basic metrics

## Operator Dashboard Principle

The operator dashboard should be included in the prototype as a value-reveal surface.

Operators may not know they need a dashboard until they see how StayReady AI transforms messy guest conversations into structured leads, readiness profiles, service requests, handoffs, incident records, post-stay follow-up opportunities, and measurable outcomes.

The dashboard should remain lightweight. It should not become a PMS, CRM, task manager, dispatch system, owner portal, staff scheduling system, or enterprise admin console.

## Six Demo Workflows

### 1. Inquiry and Lead Capture

A guest asks about availability, villa fit, services, price range, or booking interest. StayReady AI responds quickly, answers from approved content, captures key lead details, and routes qualified leads to staff.

### 2. Pre-Arrival Planning

After booking or strong booking intent, StayReady AI collects flight details, arrival time, airport transfer needs, chef preferences, grocery needs, dietary restrictions, special occasions, and other arrival-readiness information.

### 3. In-Stay Support

During the stay, StayReady AI answers common questions such as Wi-Fi, house rules, beach access, restaurant suggestions, check-out instructions, and local recommendations, while escalating complex requests.

### 4. Excursion/Activity Requests

A guest asks what to do or requests tours, restaurants, experiences, private chef dinner, spa, catamaran cruise, Dunn's River Falls, or other local activities. StayReady AI recommends approved options, captures guest interest, and routes the request to staff or a partner for confirmation.

### 5. Incident Handling and Recovery

A guest reports an issue such as an AC problem, water issue, Wi-Fi issue, maintenance concern, late driver, or urgent service problem. StayReady AI collects details, requests a photo if appropriate, tags urgency, summarizes the issue, assigns or recommends an owner, sends a safe interim response, and tracks status.

### 6. Post-Stay Follow-Up

After checkout, StayReady AI supports thank-you messages, review requests, NPS/feedback capture, repeat-stay offers, and follow-up reminders for the operator.

## MVP Boundaries

The MVP should demonstrate broad journey coverage with lightweight workflow depth.

In scope:

- Direct inquiry capture
- Website inquiry and WhatsApp-style guest experience
- Grounded responses from approved villa/property content
- Lead capture and qualification
- Structured pre-arrival intake
- Transfer, chef, grocery, dietary, and special-request capture
- In-stay support for common questions
- Excursion/activity recommendation and request capture
- Incident triage and recovery workflow
- Post-stay follow-up
- Human handoff queue
- Internal notes and AI-generated summaries
- Simple lead and request tracking
- Basic metrics

Out of scope:

- Deep PMS integration
- OTA integration
- Payment processing
- Full booking engine
- Real-time supplier inventory
- Real dispatch logic
- Supplier marketplace
- Complex multi-tenant enterprise administration
- Full guest mobile app
- Full CRM or loyalty platform
- Full maintenance management system

## Human-In-The-Loop Requirement

Human-in-the-loop escalation is a product requirement, not an optional feature.

AI may:

- Answer grounded questions from approved content
- Collect structured information
- Draft responses
- Summarize conversations
- Tag urgency
- Recommend routing
- Create internal handoff notes

Humans must handle:

- Sensitive situations
- Urgent incidents
- Ambiguous guest complaints
- High-value sales decisions
- Pricing or policy exceptions
- Emotionally loaded issues
- Anything the AI cannot answer confidently from approved content

## Proof Metrics

Potential proof metrics include:

- Median first-response time
- Percentage of inquiries answered within five minutes
- Number of direct leads captured
- Lead qualification completion rate
- Follow-up completion rate
- Percentage of arrivals with complete pre-arrival profile 48 hours before check-in
- Number of transfer requests captured
- Number of chef/grocery/dietary requests captured
- In-stay request response time
- Excursion/activity request conversion rate
- Upsell attach rate for transfers, groceries, chef services, and excursions
- Incident first-action time
- Incident resolution time
- SLA compliance rate for urgent issues
- Guest satisfaction or NPS after issue resolution
- Review request completion rate
- Post-stay follow-up completion rate
- Repeat-stay offer engagement rate
- Staff time saved
- Handoff rate
- Escalation quality
- Guest communication satisfaction

## Key Assumptions To Validate

- Premium villa operators feel enough pain in guest messaging and coordination to pilot a workflow overlay.
- Operators value response speed and lead capture enough to pay for a lightweight product before deep integrations exist.
- A dashboard makes the product more understandable and valuable rather than too heavy.
- WhatsApp-style messaging plus website inquiry is enough to demonstrate the core workflow before live WhatsApp integration.
- Human-in-the-loop controls make AI feel safer for luxury hospitality operators.
- Post-stay follow-up is useful as a lightweight workflow, not a full CRM.
- The Jamaica-first playbook can generalize to other premium villa markets without becoming a generic hotel guest platform.
